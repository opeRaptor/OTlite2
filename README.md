Obsolete Time Lite or O;TL for short, is the natural evolution of a previous project aiming to design the perfect desktop Nixie clock. 

 -Minimal design
 
 -Small form factor 
 
 -WiFi capable 
 
 -USB power 
 
 -Various atmospheric sensors.
